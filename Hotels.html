Playa Tropical Resort Hotel

Playa Tropical is a Balinese-inspired beachfront resort that allows you to drift back, connect with nature, and bask in all its glory.

Sitio Remedios Heritage Village Resort

Sitio Remedios is a beautiful resort located in Currimao, Ilocos Norte. If you are a fan of Old Buildings you will definitely fall in love with this place.

Kadilian Beach Resort

Kadilian Beach Resort is located at Ilocos Norte Brgy. Torre, Currimao, Ilocos Norte

Nipa Beach Resort

Taiwanese-run Nipa Beach Resort in Barangay Salugan which caters mostly to Chinese and local tourists.

North Shores Inn

North Shores Inn is located at Currimao Beach in Ilocos Norte. They are now offering camping which is a great way to spend a night under the stars.
